Assignment 5: Sankey

Sankey Diagram

In this last assignment , we are going to visualize a Sankey Diagram. Let's analyze it. In the first block we have stored the total Carbon Storage of the top 10 species of tree (Rounded , otherwise the links would have been too thin). In the middle of the graph there are all the kind of trees we are considering linked with a bigger links that depends on the quantity of Carbon Stored. Then , the third block represents the total Euros Saved. The links works as before. Use the tooltip that let you read all the data. Let's try to analyze the data with the support of the diagram. For example it is interesting to notice that "Cupressus" stores a great amount of Carbon but , economically speaking , they do not save so much money. On the other hand , Tilia cordata stores 659 kg of carbon and save 602 euros per year: the mathematical relationship is almost 1 to 1. So it is worth to plant this kind of tree in the future for saving more money.
