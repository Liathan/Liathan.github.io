Assignment 4: Timelines

Linechart

This plot is a combination of three different plots : two line chart representing the minimum and maximum temperature and a scatter plot representing the mean temperature. We have considered 8 different years : 1993, 1997, 2001, 2005, 2009, 2013, 2017, 2021. Each year has a particular color. Darker shade for the color representing the maximum temperature of that year, brighter shade the minimum. Every month has a maximum , minimum and mean temperature.

Radarchart

This a particular plot called radarchart. The months are radius of the circle and the smaller cirlces inside the principal one are representing the temperatures. As before , we have taken in consideration eight different years : 1993, 1997, 2001, 2005, 2009, 2013, 2017, 2021. For each year, we have displayed the mean temperature in every month.

Ridgeline

The third plot of the assignement is the ridgeline. It is displayed the ridgeline for min and max daily temperature for each month in a certain year. You can select the year you would like to analyze through the dropdown selection box below. Blu line is for minimum temperature and red is for maxiumum temperature. It is easy to understand with this plot that the minimum daily temperature are higher during summer months and lower during winter months.
